Crux home page

Welcome to the Crux information site. Crux is a suite of software tools for analyzing tandem mass spectrometry data of peptides. In these pages you will find information about installation and usage of the crux tools. The tutorials assume some understanding of mass spectrometry as it relates to analysis of peptide mixtures. Our aim is to provide a simple walk-through of a typical analysis process with sample data files provided with the programs. More advanced users may want to refer to the man pages for additional information.

Once the programs are installed, you can get started here.
